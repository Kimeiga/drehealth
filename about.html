      DRE Health Corporation is your partner in healthcare manufacturing

      DRE Health Corporation is your partner in healthcare manufacturing
      Scroll

      At DRE Health, we provide clients with the highest quality medical equipment, instruments, and supplies at the lowest prices, guaranteed. Our business was founded on the principles of quality, cost-effectiveness, selection, and unrivaled customer service — and we pride ourselves on our commitment to these principles to this day.

      DRE Health is a manufacturer, wholesaler, distributor, and retailer. We are challenging the conventional supply chain by providing an unorthodox ability for our clients to purchase products directly from us.

      Through our relationships and vast networks, we are also able to offer products manufactured by other large name brands, particularly in Laboratory & Diagnostic equipment.

      We operate manufacturing facilities in the United States, Germany, China, Pakistan, and more. Our DRE name brand instruments, equipment, and supplies are of the highest grade in precision manufacturing. All of our employees are committed to providing quick responses and consistent, high quality work. With a focus on personalized service and a 100% customer satisfaction guarantee, what are you waiting for? Contact us today.
